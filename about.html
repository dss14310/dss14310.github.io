About Us:

Not a Kit Racing is a father and son racing team formed in the spring of 2012 by Scotty and Dylan Stancil. After attending a American Motorcycle Association even at Road Atlanta the two wereeager to go fast and win big. After conducting 2 months of research it was decided that the pair would purchase a 1990 Mazda Miata and build their own Spec Miata.

The first two years were tough as the two tried to overcome the huge learning curve associated with sports car racing. In order to become a licensed race car diver you are required to go through weeks of rigorous training. In addition to this new drivers must participate in mock race events against some of the best drivers in your region who are instructed to put you in dangerous situations.

In 2014 Dylan had obtained his racing license and was eagar to win his first race. He soon found out that winning would be more difficult than driving fast. Over the next few years he studied other drivers and continued to develop his own unique driving style. In 2016 Dylan would win his first races and also come in 3rd place in the National Auto Sport Association Driver's Championship! Find upcoming races at NASA Southeast.
